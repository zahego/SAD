The official website for the hot new multiplayer rpg game, Symphony at Dawn. Participate in the ongoing project and start your adventure with more than 400 classes!!! What are you going to be? ~

Symphony at Dawn is developed as a fan lore of the songs from the American production company Two Steps from Hell. With epicness in their track, Two Steps from Hell has inspired a crazy amount of human to break out from their comfort zone and achieve the impossible. With this model in mind, the game developer hope to bring an impossible gaming experience to the audience.
